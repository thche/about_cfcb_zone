Sprouting

1990-2010
TYROL-XI-VIE
Life itself envelopes like a sprout.
Similar to the plant, we as individuals, part of our species, pass certain cycles of growth. May those beneficial towards ourselves in a positive way or not. Life itself crawls up the stairs towards success every moment. You won't see it in the moments but over time you'll see the results.

When I first started to think of what I want to became and what I want to archive in life it seemed in a retrospective point of view always pretty clear.

Pushing the boundaries of society and technologies to their maximum.

And maybe a step further.


When you are a full-time student's child and get the privilege to get in contact with laboratories and all the mind blowing findings, ideas and minds on an university in the mid 90s you somehow swallow up the energy. You get somehow enhanced by the thought that evolution through technologies could be possible. Commitments in pop- as in subcultural areas did their rest to get me hooked on that specific thought that my generation maybe can see the stars without a telescope. But the older I got. Society shaped my gem like an sandstone and my faith in the world got lost through a violent cycle of drugs, false friends and lies towards my self.
At home things started to get pretty ruff when I moved from my former base in tyrol to XI. You know classicle traumatizing childhoodd shit. Mum got a new one and Dad was not really a term i knew. Sure some of those memories and findings where shocking to me. A sentence I say by now is stated as following: When I was a child and I was left alone, i often crouched beneath the bed or in a corner and built out of my stuffed toy animals and blankets a base which gave me some sense of security, so I got friends with the shadows lurking around in these scary places, but the monsters were far more frightening." Other stuff, like seeing people doing hard drugs or hearing stories from people I used to know who ended their lifes through different circumstances are to to be honest, just part of other lives. Sure I played with the thoguhts doing the same. Sure I tried but often in the situation doing so life showed me that behind any dark corner there's some kind of lightsource.
But beside that, life had other treasures to offer.
  • Getting my first Brockhaus par example.
  • My first PC back in the days.
  • Access to the Internet.
  • Installing an OS for the first time from scrapped hardware.
  • Releasing my first website with nine.
  • Hacking my first wifi by the age of fourteen.
But to get one thing clear. Doing all this stuff, in special, learning a lot of those not so precious skills, had one thing in common..

To Survive youth!

Due of that a lot of those personal growth achievements where pretty ego-driven by that time. Because I was often described as the angry kid in the last bench which disrupts lessons in school I identified myself as a nomad of society. Through getting in touch with people in every stage of society i became more and more aware what it sometimes means to people when they say:"I'm getting through!". But on the other hand. I pretty fast learnt to not give a single damn' fuck about the opinion of others. Without ethics or a group you are part of. All of that knowledge somehow drips in something which felt for a long time like an sinkhole. But in the end, experiences in that stage of my development process are part of what I represent now.
